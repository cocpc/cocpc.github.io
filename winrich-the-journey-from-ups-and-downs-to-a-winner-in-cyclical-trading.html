Winrich: The Journey from Ups and Downs to a Winner in Cyclical Trading

Winrich

Hello everyone, I am Winrich. In this BLOG, I will share my growth journey, investment experience, and the new phase of cyclical trading. It is a story about challenges, struggles, and growth, hoping to inspire and enlighten everyone.

I started to explore futures trading in 2007. At that time, I knew nothing about investing and only had a basic understanding of the futures market. However, my curiosity and passion drove me to continue exploring, hoping to find a suitable trading approach for myself.

After years of trial and error, I arrived at October 2020, attempting to engage in trading. Due to the lack of systematic trading methods and plans, I suffered heavy losses. This experience made me realize that investment is not a simple game but an art that requires serious learning and meticulous planning.

In July 2021, I decided to enter the stage of technical advancement. I delved into the study and research of futures trading techniques, constantly trying different trading methods and strategies, learning from experience and lessons. No longer blindly following the crowd, I began to form my own trading philosophy and gradually found a suitable trading approach.

With the arrival of 2022, I experienced a psychological transformation. I learned to control emotions and mentality, becoming more composed in the face of trading fluctuations and pressures. This change in mindset not only led to better performance in trading but also made me more confident and resolute in life.

However, the above content is not the entirety of my story. In July 2023, I set foot on the trading path once again. I decided to record this new trading journey on my blog, sharing with readers every trade, every thought, and every insight. I will delve into my Cyclical Trading Principles and share the challenges and gains in trading with everyone.

This is a brand-new phase, and I know that the trading journey may still encounter setbacks and challenges. Nevertheless, I will face everything with a positive attitude. I believe that through recording and sharing my trading journey, I can become a better investor and a true winner.

Thank you for reading my BLOG! Looking forward to witnessing together, let us continuously progress and surpass on the path of investment!
