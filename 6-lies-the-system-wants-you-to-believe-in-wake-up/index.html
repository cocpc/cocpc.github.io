6 lies the System wants you to believe in... (Wake up)

6 lies the System wants you to believe in... (Wake up)
这个世界想让你相信的6个谎言...

 

1} News The news is there to spread negativity. Not to keep you informed. They want you: - Distracted - Belief there lies - Control over you Observe with your mind, not your eyes.

1}新闻

新闻就是为了牵制你的注意力，而不是为了让你了解情况。

他们希望你：

• 分散注意力

• 相信他们的谎言

• 始终受他们的控制

用你的头脑而不是你的眼睛观察。

 

2} Failure You should avoid failure, and there is only one right answer. Wrong. You need to make mistakes so that you can: - Grow - Learn - Strengthen your skills Nobody is born perfect. Embrace failure and see it as an opportunity to grow.

2} 失败

“你应该避免失败，只有一个正确的答案。”

错了。

犯错使你能：

• 成长

• 学习

• 增强你的能力

谁都不是天生完美的，接受失败，把它看作是一个成长的机会。

 

3} Work hard You should work smart, not hard. Don't get me wrong, it will still get hard sometimes, but they want you to trade your time for money your whole life. 1 Minute of your life is worth more than all money combined. Stop valuing it more than your time.

3}拼命工作

你应该工作得聪明，而不是拼命。

不要误会，有时候真的会很痛苦，但他们希望你用一生的时间换取金钱。

你的生命一分钟的价值大于所有的金钱。

 

4} Money Until 1971, money was tied to gold, which gave money value. Nowadays, money is tied to nothing. Money is basically worthless. Learn how: - To create money with no money - To make money work for you - Money works or you will work your entire life for it.

4} 金钱

1971年之前，金钱是与黄金挂钩的，这赋予了金钱价值。

现在，金钱与无物挂钩，金钱基本上毫无价值。

学会如何：

• 无钱创造金钱

• 让金钱为你工作

• 金钱运作

否则你会为金钱而一生工作。

 

5} Consumerism Many people think fulfillment lies in materialistic things. It lies in: - Personal growth - Relationships - Self-love and in contributing to something greater than ourselves. You won't find fulfillment anywhere else.

5}物质至上

许多人认为满足感在物质上。

满足感在于：

• 个人成长

• 人际关系

• 自我关爱

以及为了比我们自己更伟大的事物做贡献。

你不会在其他地方找到满足感。

6} Change is impossible “Life is the way it is” That's a lie. Life is what you make out of it. Before you can live the life you really want, you have to become the person. Change how you: - Think - Feel - Act and you can become almost anybody.

6}改变不可能

“生活就是这样的。”

这是个谎言。生活是你的创造。

在你能过上自己想要的生活之前，你必须成为一个人。

改变你的：

• 思考

• 感受

• 行动

你就能成为几乎任何人。
