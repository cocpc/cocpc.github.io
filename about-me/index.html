About Me

Winrich

Hello, everyone! I am Winrich and welcome to my blog.

My Story

I was born in a small city in central China, and I have faced various challenges and difficulties in my family. When I was around six years old, my parents lost their jobs, and then my father suffered a stroke, resulting in partial paralysis. Growing up in such an environment, I felt a sense of unease and confusion about life.

My childhood memories have become quite blurry, but I still vividly remember a moment in the fourth grade when I was punished by the teacher for being late and mocked by my classmates. It was during that time that I learned to hide my anxiety and put on a carefree facade. However, the teacher’s words - “Shake your leg in front of your disabled father” - left me disheartened and made me lose my gratitude towards the world.

During my adolescence, I went through a period of waywardness, skipping classes, and even attempting dangerous activities. After experiencing a series of setbacks, I developed an interest in computers and started learning web design and programming languages.

Investment and Trading

In 2007, I began to explore the world of futures trading, and through trial and error, I gradually understood the complexities of investment and trading. However, the initial experiences were full of failures and losses, leading me to realize that investing is not a mere game but an art that requires serious study and careful planning.

After a series of ups and downs, I grew into an investor and developed my own “Cycle Trading Rules.” During a period of introspection and contemplation, my understanding of trading and my mentality underwent a qualitative transformation.

About This Blog

This blog serves as a record of my investment and trading journey and also as a platform to share my experiences and insights with readers. Through sharing my stories and insights, I hope to motivate more people to embark on the path of investment and become better traders.

The domain name of this blog is “imwin.org,” which symbolizes “I’m a winner,” and serves as a proud declaration of my trading journey. Through this blog, I hope to connect with readers, engage in discussions about the world of investment, and grow and progress together.

About “Winrich”

“Winrich” is the English name I have chosen for myself. “Win” represents victory and success, while “rich” symbolizes wealth and abundance. For me, this name is not only a personal aspiration but also a constant reminder to maintain a positive mindset and focused spirit in my trading endeavors.

Contact Me

If you are interested in my stories and trading experiences or have any questions and suggestions, feel free to reach out to me. You can contact me via email at blog@imwin.org or find me on social media. Looking forward to connecting with you!

Thank you for visiting my blog, and I hope you find valuable information and inspiration here!
